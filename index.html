Projects

VisionSE

VisionSE is an open-sourced library of a pure-vision estimator for the motion state of an ego-vehicle. By applying optical flow measurements to consecutive image frames, such an estimator can perform motion-state estimations relying only on the input RGB data from a windshield camera attached to the vehicle.

Task-Conditional Adversarial Learning (In progress)

An adversarial learning framework for the vision-domain adaptation of end-to-end model. Our method under much stricter information-assuption outperforms the state-of-the-art methods with almost perfect information availability. Such an work is still in progress and it is expected to be published by the end of 2025.
