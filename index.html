VisionSE

VisionSE is an open-sourced library of a pure-vision estimator for the motion state of an ego-vehicle. By applying optical flow measurements to consecutive image frames, such an estimator can perform motion-state estimations relying only on the input RGB data from a windshield camera attached to the vehicle.
